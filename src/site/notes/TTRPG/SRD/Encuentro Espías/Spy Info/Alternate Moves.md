### Keiko 
>#### There's Always Work in Consultancy
>When **another Spy comes to you for advice and you tell them honestly what you think is best**, they take +1d forward when following your advice.

---

>#### Deep Deduction
>When **you are in close proximity with someone and make deductions from their appearance, behaviour and mannerisms**, roll **Wit** and choose one of the following options: surface thoughts, emotions, secrets, desires.
>
>---
>**On a 6**, you deduce something about the chosen topic;
>
>**On a 4-5**, you deduce something about the chosen topic, but they also notice you studying them.

---

### Dmitri 
>#### Hunt of the Red Dawn
>When **you Surveil while looking for a specific individual**, on a 4+ the GM will also tell you what their most recent movements here were, if any.

---

>#### Crazy Eyes 
>When **you stare someone down with your crazy eyes**, roll **Force**. 
>
>---
>**On a 4+**, they back off and leave you alone
>**On a 4-5**, choose 1 from the list below
>**On a 6**, choose 2 from the list below
>
>- *They won't tell anyone what they saw here.*
>- *They won't tell anyone how crazy you were acting.*
