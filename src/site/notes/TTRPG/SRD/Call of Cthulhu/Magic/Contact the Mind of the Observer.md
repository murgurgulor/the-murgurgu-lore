> # Contact the Mind of the Observer
> ##### Details 
>**Magic Points:** 4
>**Sanity:** 1D3
>**Casting Time:** PT5M
>
> ###### Spell Description 
> Casting this spell contacts a member of the race of Yith that is closest by on this plane of existence.
> It initially comes back as a ping, after which the Yith - if interested - will seek out the caster to see what their intentions are.
>
